About Aurora Studies

What We Do

Aurora Studies is a student-run platform that creates academic research articles into bite-sized, practical articles. We focus on topics that actually matter to students such as topics that can be used in subjects. — and present the work and conclusions in clear, jargon-free language.

Every article is backed by evidence and includes easy citation tools, but we skip the academic complexity and get straight to what you need to know.

Why We Started

There’s a lot of resources out there for students, but not all of it is backed by evidence and is actually student led. We wanted to create a space where students can find trustworthy, research-based answers to common questions without swimming through dense academic papers or clickbait articles without clear statistics

Whether you’re trying to figure out if you should pull an all-nighter, wondering if exercise really helps with focus, or just looking for better study strategies or you need some citations for your graded piece of work, We got you covered.

Our Approach

  • Evidence-based: Every claim is backed by peer-reviewed research and real life proof given.
  • Student-friendly: We write in student-friendly language, as we are students!
  • Practical: Our articles actually contain information useful to your day to day life!
  • Honest: If the research is a bit too crazy or complex, we will make a simplfied version of it, you get access to both for free.
